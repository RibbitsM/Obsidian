The rivers run like blood through the earth  
carrying with them the dreams of my elders  
the food of our freedom.  
For this Bío Bío  
is trapped within dams.  
FUXALEUFU (another river in Chile)  
You who carries the breath of the monkey puzzles.  
You who tells the stories in  
the LAFQUENMAPU (mythical "land across the water")  
and relay the message of the snow  
in the freezing tears of winter  
that pour down on my brothers.  
For this we covet  
Bío Bío, the great river.  
The foreigners  
don't see the pulse of our history  
in your waters  
they want to stop your song  
and silence the voices of our ancestors.  
Condors of the sun kiss your waters  
and my ancestors rise.
