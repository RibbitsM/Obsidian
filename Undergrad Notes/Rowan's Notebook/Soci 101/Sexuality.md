Ghosting

- Ghosting is the practice of ending a relationship by cutting contact with them without explanation
- The usual justification for this is to avoid the pain and stress of a breakup
- People consider it less cruel than a traditional breakup
- 30% of people said they ghosted someone
- Not just a term for dating, can be applied to many relationships
- The volume of choice for potential dates can numb people
- Decreased consequences for ghosting someone, no other social connection to them
- Also easier to cut people off online than in real life
- Ghosting is era specific
- Meeting people outside of our social circle makes ghosting easier
- Has existed throughout human history but has increased dramatically because of technology
- Not necessarily ethical on unethical
- Can sometimes be safe and justified, sometimes causes more harm than good
- Important to consider the benefit of forcing yourself to have an uncomfortable conversation
