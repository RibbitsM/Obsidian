- Marginal Decision Making
- Ideal model for consumers to use
- MU of good X over price of good X compared with MU of good y over price of good Y
- Lets you see which good gives you greater utility per dollar spent
- When MU of X goes up, MU of Y goes down and vice versa
- Constant cycle because MU diminishes over time
- Optimal consumption is when X and Y balance out
- Ties in to viewing S and D curves as marginal utility curves  
- Marginal benefit of X is maximized at Qo and Po
- After certain point, MB of X starts declining and MB of Y increases
