Product Rule

- How do you find the probability of winning the Lotto 6/49
- You need to match 6 balls out of 49
- 6/49 x 5/48 x 4/47 x 3/46 x 2/45 x 1/44
- 1 in 14 million dollars
- Every time a result is revealed, you remove that result from the pool
- Ex: you guessed 7,8,12,34, 26, 27 and the first draw is 12, remove 12 from your pool of guesses and the pool of draws, now the odds are 5/48
- If you flip two coins, they can be HH (both heads), TT (both tails), or HT (heads and tails)
- Half the time it will be HT, a quarter HH, and a quarter TT
- No matter how many coins you flip, multiply the amount of coins by the amount of sides to find the probability
- 8 dice with 6 sides has 64 possible outcomes
- Same with combining genotypes and phenotypes
- Say that the G gene makes a plant green and the g makes it yellow
- A breed between GgTt and TtGg would create a 9:3:3:1 ratio
- Most would be green and tall, some would be green and short or yellow and tall and only a few would be yellow and short
- Called a dihybrid cross
- Each gene has 4 possibilities and each plant has a set of 4
- Therefore we get 16 possible genotypes
