**Gestalt Principles**

- Similarity means that people tend to group together things that look similar
- Proximity means that people assume that things that are closer together are connected
- Connectedness means that we group elements that are connected by lines, colors, shapes, frames, etc.
- Enclosure means drawing attention to a group of objects by separating them via a box or circle
- Another feature of enclosure is mentally assuming concealed shapes are whole
- Simply by putting a label close to an object, we associate that object with a label
- Continuity means that we assume geometric objects to be continuous, similar to the feature of enclosure
